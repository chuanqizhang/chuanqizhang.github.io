1.3. Evaluation
As we are aiming at continuous regression instead of dis-
crete classification, MSE could be a better metric to evaluate
rather than accuracy analysis. Furthermore, bias-variance
decomposition will more specifically indicate the factor of
whether the model is success or not. From we have dis-
cussed in class, a high bias can show the model’s disabil-
ity to learn relations between features effectively and hence
would underfit on the dataset leading to low accuracy while
predicting. By contrast, a high variance indicates the pre-
dicted output become highly flexible with respect to the data
points of the dataset, which causes a model to overfit on the
training data leading to low accuracy while predicting. So
they are the key points considered for measuring success in
our project, and we calculate according to the formulas as

ent Boosting in Tree Algorithm, which uses base regression
