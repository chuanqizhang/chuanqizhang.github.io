1 Introduction
EOF Empirical Orthogonal Function (EOF) is a powerful methodology for analyzing the structure of
the data matrix so as to replace the raw data by their principal components. In the EOF method, we
will make a decomposition of the data matrix into time series and spatial patterns.
SSA Singular Spectrum Analysis (SSA) is a well-developed methodology of time series analysis and
forecasting, which comprises many dierent but inter-linked methods. The basic SSA method consists
of two complementary stages: decomposition and reconstruction; both stages include two separate steps.
At the rst stage we decompose the series and at the second stage we reconstruct the original series and
use the reconstructed series for forecasting new data points.
