Chuanqi Zhang czhang578@wisc.edu
Abstract SSA is a powerful methodology in time series analysis which consists of two complementary
stages - decomposition and reconstruction. In this report, I will focus on how to perform and explain the
two with the time-lagged embedding matrix, then show the optimality at the end.

In SSA, we always use SVD (Singular Value Decomposition) to decompose the time-lagged embedding
matrix. In order to make the theories more clear, I will introduce the decomposition approach in two
